What Makes Tim Hortons Donuts So Popular?

Tim Hortons has been serving donuts since 1964, and there's a reason the lineup at the drive-through never seems to shrink. The donuts are made fresh daily, they're affordable, and they come in a variety that covers every craving — from classic glazed rings to indulgent cream-filled options.

Tim Hortons also understands the value of consistency. Whether you're in Vancouver, Toronto, or Halifax, you can walk in and expect the same Honey Dip or Old Fashioned Plain that you've always loved. That reliability is a huge part of the brand's loyal customer base.

Full Tim Hortons Donuts Menu: Every Flavour Listed

Here's a complete breakdown of Tim Hortons donuts currently available on the Canadian menu:

Classic Ring Donuts

  • Honey Dip — A light yeast ring dipped in a sweet honey glaze. This is arguably the most iconic Tim Hortons donut and a top seller year after year.
  • Old Fashioned Plain — A dense, cake-style donut with a slightly crispy exterior. No icing, no fuss — just classic donut flavour.
  • Old Fashioned Glazed — Same cake-style base as the plain, but with a smooth sugar glaze on top.
  • Chocolate Dip — A yeast ring dipped in chocolate icing. A simple but beloved choice for chocolate fans.
  • Chocolate Glazed — Similar to the chocolate dip but with a thicker, richer chocolate coating.
  • Maple Dip — A yeast ring with a sweet maple-flavoured glaze. A Canadian classic that perfectly complements a double-double.
  • Dutchie — A square-shaped donut filled with raisins and topped with a sugar glaze. Old-school and underrated.

Filled Donuts

For those who want something a little more indulgent, Tim Hortons filled donuts deliver a punch of flavour in every bite.

  • Canadian Maple — A filled donut stuffed with maple cream and topped with maple-flavoured icing. This one is a must-try for anyone who hasn't had it yet.
  • Strawberry Vanilla — Filled with strawberry jam and topped with vanilla icing and rainbow sprinkles. A fan favourite, especially with kids.
  • Boston Cream — Filled with smooth vanilla custard and topped with chocolate fondant icing. One of the best-selling donuts on the entire menu.
  • Chocolate Cream Filled — A chocolate cake-style donut filled with cream and dusted with cocoa. Perfect for double-chocolate lovers.
  • Blueberry Filled — A yeast donut filled with blueberry jam and dusted with powdered sugar. Light and fruity.
  • Raspberry Filled — Similar to the blueberry, but with a sweet raspberry jam filling.
  • Lemon Filled — A light yeast donut filled with lemon cream. Tangy and refreshing.

Timbiebs & Timbits (Mini Donuts)

No Tim Hortons donut guide is complete without mentioning Timbits — those bite-sized donut holes that are dangerously easy to eat by the dozen.

Popular Timbit flavours include:

  • Old Fashioned Plain
  • Honey Dip
  • Chocolate Glazed
  • Birthday Cake (seasonal)
  • Strawberry
  • Blueberry

Timbits come in boxes of 10, 20, 40, and 50, making them perfect for groups, parties, or road trips.
